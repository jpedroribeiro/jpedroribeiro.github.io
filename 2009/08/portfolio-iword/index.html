Portfolio: iWord

August 24, 2009

In this project I was requested to not only redesign their current page but to come up with 2 animated banner ads, which will be featured on the main portals in Ireland. The company, iWord is specialized in mobile messaging solution and has been on the market since 2004.

Web Site

Based on the logo designed by my good friend Daniel Falcão, the web site has a big area explaining the 3 easy steps to create your account with iWord and start using. Below this area, the secondary links to content pages.
